description: Generate a yearly-memory draft from the year's monthly entries
argument-hint: "[YYYY]"
---

Run `~/.claude/skills/chrono-palace-memory/tools/aggregate-yearly.py $ARGUMENTS`.

Yearly is the highest-level layer — be concise. Focus on:
1. **Project trajectories**: which projects were active when, started/completed/abandoned, with key outcomes.
2. **Stable preferences**: preferences that solidified vs. those that drifted.
3. **3–5 most consequential decisions** of the year (not all decisions — just the ones with lasting impact).
4. **Long-term open loops** carried over to next year.

The yearly file should fit on one screen. Do NOT copy-paste daily content into it.

After saving, consider archival policy:
- Daily entries from very old months can be `status: archived` if their content is already promoted to palace
- Monthly entries are the canonical summary for each month going forward
- Yearly entries are the canonical summary for each year

Update MEMORY.md with the yearly link.
